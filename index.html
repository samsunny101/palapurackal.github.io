ABOUT

    • We are a retired couple who is running the PALAPURACKAL HOMESTAY, which is located in a quiet village center of Camorino, near historical city center of Bellinzona (canton Ticino).


      Bellinzona Train Station is just 10 minutes away by car, 4.1 km ride to the well known Castles of Bellinzona and 5 minutes of walk to nearest catholic church. Other well known attractions like Lugano city , Locarno city and Ponte Tibetano (Tibetan Bridge) are easly reachable from here.


      PALAPURACKAL HOMESTAY offers rooms with free WiFi, One toilet and one bathroom with shower, hairdryer. Public TV is available, as well as Gym, free private parking and a big garden. On request sightseeing will be arranged for you (Extra payment).


      Luggage storage is available and if you are interested in Ayurvedic Massage, we will be happy to arrange it for you. (Ps: Ayurvedic Massage is not Free). Breakfast is included in price (free) and Kerala food are available but need to be booked in advance.


      We can arrange day trips to scenic mountain villages and experience an authentic Switzerland away from the crowded touristic attractions. The serene valleys with grazing cows and spectacular snow-capped mountains are all within a short drive from our property.
